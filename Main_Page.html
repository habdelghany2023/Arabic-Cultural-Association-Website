 What is the Arabic Club?

 The Arab Student Club of UMass is a non-profit student organization that works on enhancing awareness of Arabic cultural heritage, and creating a close community among Arab students and their friends.

 Our Mission...

 To celebrate Arabic cultural heritage and share it with the rest of the local community.

 Our Club Objectives...

  • To increase awareness on issues pertinent to the Arab World and the Arab communities in the United States.
  • To offer moral and social support for Arab and Arab American students.
  • To promote multiculturalism by working with other student organizations in a collective effort to enhance cross cultural understanding.
